HEALING ROOT AGRO VENTURES

About Us

Who We Are

Healing Root Agro Ventures is a leading agricultural enterprise dedicated to providing smallholder and commercial farmers with superior-quality planting materials and practical farm solutions. We combine years of agronomic expertise with innovative agricultural practices to ensure farmers achieve higher yields, healthier crops, and sustainable income growth. Our commitment to excellence and reliability has made us a trusted partner across Nigeria’s farming communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers by supplying improved crop varieties, reliable seedlings, and expert guidance, ensuring that every farm achieves its maximum productive potential. We strive to make agriculture both profitable and sustainable, equipping farmers with tools, knowledge, and resources needed for better yield, market access, and long-term growth. Healing Root Agro Ventures is committed to elevating Nigerian agriculture to international standards.

Our Vision

We envision a future where farmers across Nigeria and beyond can access the best planting materials and guidance to secure food, income, and sustainable farming practices. Our goal is to become the country’s most trusted agricultural partner, fostering modern farming techniques, supporting rural development, and enabling small and large-scale farmers alike to thrive through improved yields, efficient production, and high-quality crops.

What We Offer

We provide a wide range of superior agricultural products, including TME 419 cassava stems improved by IITA, hybrid plantain and banana suckers, Tenera oil palm seedlings, coconut and pineapple seedlings, high-quality vegetable seeds, and NPK fertilizers. Beyond products, we offer personalized farm consultancy, planting guidance, and post-sale support to help farmers optimize their land, reduce crop loss, and achieve higher productivity and profitability.

Why Farmers Choose Us

Farmers choose Healing Root Agro Ventures because of our commitment to quality, reliability, and results. We supply disease-free, high-yield planting materials with transparent pricing, on-time delivery, and practical farm guidance. Our products are selected for resilience, early maturity, and market relevance, ensuring farmers can achieve superior harvests. Our dedication to customer support and agricultural excellence makes us a preferred partner nationwide.

Meet the Founder

Olasupo Segun Victor, the founder, has extensive experience in modern agriculture and agribusiness. Passionate about sustainable farming, he envisioned a company that delivers high-quality seedlings, fertilizers, and farm solutions with a focus on maximizing farmer success. Under his guidance, Healing Root Agro Ventures ensures clients receive reliable products, professional advice, and support, empowering farmers to increase productivity and income consistently.
