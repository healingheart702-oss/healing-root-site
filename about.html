About Healing Root Agro Ventures

Healing Root Agro Ventures is dedicated to providing high-quality agricultural products and expert farming services to help farmers and agripreneurs succeed. We specialize in cassava stems, hybrid plantain, oil palm seedlings, coconut and pineapple seedlings, okra and vegetable seeds, as well as NPK fertilizers.

Our mission is to empower farmers with the best quality seedlings and support sustainable agriculture through training, consultancy, and farm setup services.

Vision: To become the leading provider of agricultural solutions that nurture tomorrow's farms and communities.
