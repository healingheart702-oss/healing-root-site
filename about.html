About Healing Root Agro Ventures

Growing Quality. Nurturing Tomorrow.

Who We Are

Healing Root Agro Ventures is a fast-growing agricultural enterprise based in Moniya, Oyo State, Nigeria. Founded by Oluwasegun Victor, the company was established with a simple mission: to make premium-quality agricultural planting materials and farm solutions available to farmers across Nigeria at affordable prices.

Over the years, Healing Root Agro Ventures has earned a strong reputation for trust, transparency, and professionalism. What began as a small seed distribution initiative has now evolved into a comprehensive agro-supply and consultancy brand helping farmers improve yield, reduce losses, and achieve sustainable success.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with high-quality seedlings, expert guidance, and reliable agricultural inputs that enhance productivity, increase profitability, and support long-term food security in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To become one of Nigeria’s most trusted agro-supply brands by continuously delivering excellence, innovation, and value to farmers at all levels—from smallholders to large-scale commercial growers.

Why Farmers Choose Us

At Healing Root Agro Ventures, we don’t just sell seedlings—we deliver value. Our planting materials are carefully sourced, disease-resistant, and tested for high yield. We prioritize honesty, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction. Every farmer who buys from us becomes part of our growing community of success-driven agricultural partners.

Our Commitment to Farmers

We are committed to helping you grow. From seed selection to planting, field management, and harvesting strategies, we walk with you through every stage. Our approach combines traditional farming knowledge with modern agronomic techniques to produce optimal results for every customer.

Meet the Founder

Oluwasegun Victor is a passionate agricultural entrepreneur from Moniya, Oyo State. His dedication to empowering Nigerian farmers inspired the creation of Healing Root Agro Ventures.
